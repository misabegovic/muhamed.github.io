We are living through a shift from the web as a place to the web as a feed. The implications for memory and attention are enormous.

I miss the web as a place. A blog you visited, a forum you checked, a corner of the internet that felt like it had geography. Now most of my online time is infinite vertical feeds optimized for engagement, not memory. The result is a kind of ephemerality that makes it hard to build up a body of work or a body of thought.

Part of why I am rebuilding this site is to resist that. I want a corner that accumulates.
