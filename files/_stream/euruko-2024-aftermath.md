Hosting EuRuKo was a masterclass in distributed trust. The Ruby community runs on goodwill and careful curation.

Months later, I am still processing what worked and what did not. The obvious things — venue, schedule, food — matter less than I expected. The thing people remember is whether they felt welcome, whether they met someone unexpected, whether the hallway conversations were worth the trip.

I want to write a longer retrospective, but the short version is: optimize for connection, not polish.
